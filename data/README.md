* f_medium_sessions

  * profileId (site id)
  * medium (channel being used to contact the targeted audience)
  * sessions (clicks)
* f_event_sessions
  * dt (activity date)
  * profileId (site id)
  * medium (channel being used to contact the targeted audience)
  * event_category
  * landing_pagepath
  * country
  * sessions
  * users
